Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about working with WebCraft Studio

How long does it take to build a website?

+

Typically, a starter website takes 2-3 weeks, a business website takes 4-6 weeks, and e-commerce or custom projects take 8-12 weeks. Timeline depends on project complexity and content readiness.

Do you provide content and images?

+

We can work with your existing content or help source professional stock photos. Copywriting services are available as an add-on for an additional fee.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

+

Absolutely! All our websites are fully responsive and optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop devices. We test on all major browsers and screen sizes.

Can I update the website myself after launch?

+

Yes! We build websites with user-friendly content management systems (CMS) and provide training so you can easily update text, images, and pages without technical knowledge.

What if I need changes after the website is live?

+

All packages include free support (1-6 months depending on package). After that, we offer affordable maintenance plans or hourly rates for updates and changes.

Do you offer hosting and domain registration?

+

Yes! We can handle hosting setup and domain registration, or work with your existing hosting provider. We'll recommend the best solution for your needs and budget.
